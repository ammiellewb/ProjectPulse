About


At ProjectPulse, we understand that the heartbeat of any successful project is effective management and seamless communication. Our taskboard is engineered to provide an intuitive, real-time overview of your project's progress.

With ProjectPulse, you can easily create, assign, and track tasks, ensuring that your project stays on track and on time. Key features:

  • To-do Lists: Add a to-do list to any project and organize tasks into manageable lists.
  • Task Handling: Add tasks to your to-do lists, view specific task details, edit tasks to update their status or content, and delete tasks as they are completed or canceled.
  • File Management: Attach files to tasks or notes, ensuring all relevant documents are easily accessible and manageable within the project environment.
  • Notes: Keep track of important ideas and meetings. Browse through a list of all notes, view detailed contents, and edit notes to reflect new insights.
